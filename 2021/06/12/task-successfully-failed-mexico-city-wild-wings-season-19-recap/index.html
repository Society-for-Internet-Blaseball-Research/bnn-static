Task Successfully Failed: Mexico City Wild Wings Season 19 Recap

By: Spludge237

In Season 16, Summers scored 17% of the Wings’ runs while only taking up 10% of the plate appearances, and led the team in almost all batting statistics. In summation, the Mexico City Wild Wings‚Äô Season 19 was the story of clever planning being executed well to not the result wanted due to the caprice of Blaseball in general, which when phrased like that makes it very much like most seasons for most teams in this great splort.
